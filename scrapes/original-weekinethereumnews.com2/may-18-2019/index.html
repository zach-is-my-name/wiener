May 18, 2019

Ethereum News and Links

Layer 1

  • Using Eth2 as an Eth1 finality gadget
  • Everything you need to know about Eth2 through phase 2
  • My phase 0 roadmap summary: individual clients already have testnets to varying degrees. Spec freeze end of June, networking this summer, longstanding cross-client testnet early Q4, auditing/testing, go live around end of q4 or q1
  • Exploration of discouragement attacks

Layer 2

  • Matter doing over “100 TPS of ZK transactions on ETH mainnet” but couldn’t test higher because they crashed Digital Ocean
  • Separating the role of signatures in transaction validity vs slashing
  • Mainnet sneak peak of Celer Network at EthNY
  • Decentraland adopts Matic for fast and cheap layer2 sales of Estates
  • Raiden light client SDK and app, live on Rinkeby

Client releases

  • Parity v2.5.1-beta or v2.4.6-stable

Stuff for developers

  • Solidity Hot Reloading using Zepkit
  • Pocketh: a “pocket knife for auditing smart contracts” from Zeppelin
  • Skale explainer on using their sidechain storage as an IPFS alternative
  • An sbt-ethereum tutorial
  • Sandcastle, an Eth SQL language pre-alpha available in Remix.
  • Slither v0.6.4
  • Drizzle v1.3.0
  • Build a simple insurance Etherisc product with role-based actors
  • Enjin’s Java SDK to integrate a blockchain economy into your Minecraft server

Ecosystem

  • Swarm v0.4.0 released
  • POA Network is picking up the Kovan testnet and changing it to 4 second block times.

Enterprise

  • EEA enterprise client spec v3. Plus EEA released trusted compute spec v1.0
  • World Bank and Commonwealth Bank of Australia trade 9 figure secondary bond built on Ethereum
  • GE Aviation to track aircraft engine parts maintenance an use with a consortium chain built on Ethereum
  • Aura: a product tracking consortium chain for the luxury industry from LVMH, ConsenSys and Microsoft
  • Element implementation of Decentralized Identity Foundation’s Sidetree Protocol

Governance and Standards

  • Proposed list of EIPs for Istanbul hard fork
  • EIP2028: Calldata gas cost reduction
  • ERC2009: Compliance service
  • EIP2029: State Rent A – State counters contract
  • EIP2031: State Rent B – Net transaction counter
  • EIP2027: State Rent C – Net contract size accounting
  • EIP2026: State Rent H – Fixed Prepayment for accounts
  • EIP2035: Stateless Clients – Repricing SLOAD and SSTORE to pay for block proofs
  • ERC2015: Wallet Update Chain JSON-RPC Method (`wallet_updateChain`)
  • Nexus Mutual: digital cooperatives are the future

Application layer

  • iExec v3: secure “data renting,” particularly aimed at enterprises. Massive French utility company EDF to use it.
  • Sell your personal Firefox info through Streamr
  • The CryptoKitties folks debut a new game called CheezeWizards
  • Cryptocup returns for CopaAmerica – predict South America’s cup
  • Augur v1 cutoff on September 15th. Also interesting: Veil forked Augur, though they say they’re coming back for v2
  • 100m USD in loans outstanding on DeFi
  • Maker Stability Fee lowered to 17.5%
  • District0x launches MemeFactory. Curation markets, TCRs. Memes.

Interviews, Podcasts, Videos, Talks 

  • Ethereal videos
  • Fluidity Summit vidos
  • Gitcoin’s Kevin Owocki on Zero Knowledge
  • Argent’s Itamar Lesuisse on Into the Ether
  • Aya Miyaguchi on Epicenter

Tokens / Business / Regulation

  • Katie Haun: what’s going on in the fight between Kik and the SEC
  • Farewell words from outgoing CFTC Chair Giancarlo

General

  • Fast Fourier Transforms explainer
  • Flexa and Gemini announce some major retails chains to accept ETH and BTC
  • Stellar crashed for 2 hours and no one noticed
  • A cryptocurrency wallet is not a wallet! If you didn’t know this, click the link.
