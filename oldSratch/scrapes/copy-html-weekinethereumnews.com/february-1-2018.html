February 1, 2018

Ethereum News and Links

Protocol and client release

  • Plasma implementation call #1
  • Core dev call. Agenda.
  • Karl Floersch on Casper FFG talk at BPASE
  • Initial explorations on taking Casper FFG to full PoS
  • Bjorn and Max from Parity talk to Giveth about bridging chains
  • Parity v1.9: performance tuning release.  faster warp sync, less hard drive usage, less bandwidth due to snappy


Stuff for developers

  • Bernhard Mueller: Using formal verification to avoid bugs
  • Matthew Di Ferrante: Mechanism Design Security against frontrunning and malicious wrappers
  • CryptoZombies lesson 4
  • Lots of new stuff in Web3.py v4
  • AragonOS 3.0 alpha

Ecosystem

  • EEA welcomes 80 more members, including AMD, EY, Pfizer, RBC.
  • Infura relayed over 7 million ETH in 2017
  • Mark Beylin: creating standards for Ethereum bounties.  Bounties Network, Gitcoin, and Status all working together.  
  • EthGasStation: best predictions by using both mined transactions and the transaction pool
  • Owning an ENS domain can be as easy as buying a DNS name.
  • Ayako Miyaguchi is the new Exec Director of the Ethereum Foundation.
  • Status invests $5m in Riot.im/Matrix, which will cross-integrate messaging and dapps using the Status token

Project Updates

  • The Augur white paper
  • KyberNetwork to launch on Feb 11 for original KGT buyers
  • Cofound.it q4 report
  • Spankchain – payment channels, working on streaming video
  • FunFair – working on FateChannels, getting ready for ICE, working on regulatory applications
  • Iconomi Q4 financial report
  • Maker: The road ahead for Dai
  • Melonport quarterly – going live on mainnet in February with bug bounty.  Their manager competition is live on the Kovan testnet

Interviews, Podcasts, Videos, Talks

  • Zeppelin’s Manuel Araoz on Epicenter
  • I talk to Ben Siegel from Blockchain for Social Impact on how non-profits can use Ethereum
  • A16z podcast: Nick Tomaino and Chris Dixon talk tokens
  • How Fabian Vogelsteller wants to change the fashion industry with Lukso
  • Let’s Talk ETC: Everett Hildenbrandt talks KEVM
  • Kevin Owocki talks Gitcoin on the Changelog
  • Joe Lubin talk at Davos
  • Rhys Lindmark talks to ConsenSys Token Foundry’s Harrison Hines
  • Maciej Olpinksi talk on Userfeeds

Tokens

  • Fabric Ventures: State of Token Data
  • Arsenal Football Club getting into the ICO shilling game.
  • OMERS (Ontario govt employees retirement) linked to $50m capital raise to invest in the Ethereum ecosystem
  • CFTC subpoenaed Bitfinex/Tether on Dec 6th though it just became public this week.
  • Bitfinex now trades all pairs against ETH

General

  • The big 60000 acre land purchase at the Tahoe-Reno Industrial Center was from the Berns, the publishers of ETHNews, to showcase what Ethereum makes possible.  Nice teaser.
  • Brian Armstrong proposes a cryptocharity that would help give people property rights
  • Katy Perry put crypto logos on her nails. Ethereum got the thumbs.
  • ConsenSys and Amalto developing the Ondiflo platform for oilfield services logistics
  • Hyperledger went to 1.0 with its second product, Sawtooth, which runs the EVM
  • NASA is in very early research stages of considering Ethereum for resiliency
