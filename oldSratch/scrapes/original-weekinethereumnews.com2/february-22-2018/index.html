February 22, 2018

Ethereum News and Links

Protocol

  • Li Xuanji presentation of generalized state channels at EthDenver
  • Video from Jon Choi’s Casper cryptoeconomics talk
  • web3 Foundation: investigating short-term scalability solutions through PoA bridgechains.  Also about Witnet’s sidechains and bridge.
  • Latest Casper standup
  • Matt Condon: scaling solutions and tradeoffs

Stuff for developers

  • Drizzle: reactive Ethereum data for front-ends from Truffle
  • dharma.js – JavaScript Library for Dharma Protocol
  • Remix desktop app
  • Espresso – Solidity test framework
  • What Colony has learned about securing local storage.  tl;dr it’s early days
  • Storage Allocation Exploits in Solidity
  • Analysis of Mythril and Oyente
  • Two different analyses: 1) Dissecting the honeypot from last week and 2) exposing Ethereum honeypots.  Plus a safe demonstration of the honeypot using Truffle
  • Overflow – tooling platform from MyCrypto team
  • ERC892: Permission standard
  • Nikolic, et al: Finding The Greedy, Prodigal, and Suicidal Contracts at Scale

Bugfix releases

  • Geth v1.8.1
  • Parity v1.9.3

Ecosystem

  • Ethereum Community Fund. $100m from Cosmos, OmiseGo, Golem, Maker, Global Brain, and Raiden to support ecosystem infrastructure.
  • First part of bounty paid for the foundation of the Dogethereum bridge
  • Colorado Coin – the EthDenver mealtracker has now become an ERC20 token
  • de la Rouviere: TCRs to protect the commons
  • kvhnuke’s reincarnation of MyEtherWallet
  • The Stable Fund from L4/Maker. $45 million to support the Maker ecosystem, starting with their ’request for startups’ page.

Project Announcements

  • Cellarius: Genesis Thought. Here’s the trailer or read the vision
  • EthDenver submissions
  • XLNT – Matt Condon’s cryptocollectibles work

Interviews, Podcasts, Videos, Talks

  • Archive of video streams from EthDenver
  • Taylor Monahan on Unconfirmed. Olaf on Laura’s first episode.
  • Jackson Palmer interviews FOAM’s Ryan John King
  • Put your CryptoKitties on your Firefly DIY hardware wallet
  • Epicenter: 0x and Multicoin Capital
  • Conrad Barski interviewed on blockchains in healthcare

Tokens

  • CFTC issues a warning about pump and dumps.  You can also blow the whistle on them and get a reward.
  • Vitalik’s signaling theory model of cryptocurrency issuance and value
  • Swiss Finma issues “ICO Guidelines” delineating 3 types: payment, utliity, and asset.
  • Alex Felix: Fundamentals of Discount Tokens

General

  • Chris Dixon: why decentralization matters
  • The Inside Story of the CryptoKitties Congestion Crisis
  • World Food Programme saving millions in bank fees by using Ethereum

Dates of note

Upcoming dates:

  • Mar 8-10 – Ethereum Community Conference in Paris
  • May 3-5 – Edcon in Toronto
